I'm a first-year tech student with a burning curiosity for both the present and the past. While I'm deeply engrossed in the ever-evolving world of technology, my heart holds a special place for the rich treasure of ancient Bharatiya science, literature, and art. From the strategic genius of Arthashastra to the celestial insights of Surya Siddhanta, and the profound philosophies of Advaita and Dvaita Vedanta, these ancient treasures continue to inspire and illuminate.

My fascination extends beyond mere knowledge. I see immense potential in bridging the gap between these seemingly disparate fields. I believe that by understanding the wisdom of our ancestors, we can fuel innovation in the present and create a future that is both technologically advanced and deeply rooted in our cultural heritage.
